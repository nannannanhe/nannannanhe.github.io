Musicboard + Blog, 2020 HTML · CSS · JS · MySQL · WordPress

Maintaining scrapers across venues became unsustainable after starting full-time work. Rebuilt around a different idea: Musicboard spotlighted events I wanted to promote by pairing each one with a handpicked track, so visitors could discover the show and the artist's music at the same time. The blog covered everyday life in Sendai — favourite eateries, recycling guides, beach cleanups — and lyric translations from Traditional Chinese into Japanese.
